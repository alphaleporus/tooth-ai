MODEL PLACEHOLDER

This directory should contain:
- maskrcnn_final.pth (Mask R-CNN trained model)
- effnet_fdi_final.pth (EfficientNet classifier model)
- config.yaml (Detectron2 configuration)

To obtain models:
1. Train models in Phase 3 (Mask R-CNN) and Phase 4 (EfficientNet)
2. Copy trained models:
   cp workspace/phase3/exp_1024/model_1024_final.pth workspace/tooth-ai/models/maskrcnn_final.pth
   cp workspace/phase4/effnet_fdi_best.pth workspace/tooth-ai/models/effnet_fdi_final.pth
   cp workspace/configs/mask_rcnn_1024x512.yaml workspace/tooth-ai/models/config.yaml

WARNING: Inference will fail without actual model files.
